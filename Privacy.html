Privacy Policy

Froom stores team status data (name, role, status, note, location, timestamps) per practice inside your own Firebase project. We do not collect analytics or share data with third parties.

Data we process

  • Display name and role
  • Status, note, and location
  • Update timestamps and the user who made the change

Retention

Data remains in your Firebase project until you delete it.

Security

Access to write data requires Firebase Authentication. Use strict Firestore rules for production.

Contact

Practice managers can contact the site owner to request data removal.
